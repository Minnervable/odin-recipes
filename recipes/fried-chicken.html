Fried Chicken

How to make fried chicken

Fried chicken is true comfort food.

When done right it is not only a delicacy, but a delicious weeknight dinner.

Follow these simple steps and your belly will thank you!

What You Need

  • 2 pounds chicken tenderloins
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Spices
    • Oregano
    • Thyme
    • Rosemary
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 3 cups oil
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch

What to Do

  1. Fill a deep skillet with the oil and set the stove on medium-high heat
  2. Prepare your breading station:
    • In a shallow dish, such as a pie plate, combine the flour, cornstarch, breadcrumbs, and spices.
    • In a similar dish, combine the eggs and buttermilk
  3. Bread each piece of chicken individually starting with the egg bath, then the breading. Place the pieces on a plate until you are ready to put them in the oil.
  4. Once you have enough chicken to fill your pan, add them to the oil and cook them on each side for 4-6 minutes.
  5. Remove them from the pan and put the pieces on a plate with a layer of paper towels and let them rest for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Dig in and enjoy!
