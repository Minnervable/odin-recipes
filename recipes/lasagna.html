Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasagna is a family friendly recipe.

You don't have to be a five star chef to make it.

Follow along and you will have a delicious dinner in less than two hours.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 can canned tomatoes
  • 32 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Boil the pasta according to the box
  3. Brown the ground beef.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and half the italian seasoning to the skillet with the beef.
  5. In a large bowl combine: all of the ricotta cheese, both eggs, a cup of the mozzarella cheese, and the remaining italian seasoning.
  6. Grease a 9x13 baking dish. The layers will go as follows: beef and tomato mixture, pasta, cheese mixture, repeat.
  7. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes.
