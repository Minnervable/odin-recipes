Shepherds Pie

The Best and Easiest Shepherd's Pie

Shepherd's Pie is savory and filling.

It may seem complicated or out of reach for you, however, its only three simple layers.

Let me walk you through the best way to make it!

List of Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 2 15oz. cans of peas and carrots
  • 6 large russet potatoes
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • 1 packet Lipton 'Onion Soup' mix
  • Spices
    • salt
    • pepper
    • paprika
    • garlic powder
    • onion powder
    • ground mustard
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

How to Make It

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Boil 6 qts of water for the potatoes. Heat the oil in a 12in. skillet.
  3. Brown the beef and the sausage together in the skillet with the oil.
  4. Open the cans of peas and carrots and drain them. Set aside
  5. Once the water is boiling, put the potatoes in and boil them until they are fork tender
  6. Drain the meat. Add the spices and the Lipton packet to the meat.
  7. Mash the potatoes and add the milk.
  8. Grease a 9x13 baking dish.

    9. How to Assemble

    1. The meat mixture on the bottom
    2. The peas and carrots on top of the meat.
    3. The mashed potatoes on top of the pease and carrots
    4. Top it all off with cheese
  9. Bake in oven covered for 20 minutes.
  10. Uncover and bake for another 15 minutes or until the top is brown and bubbly
